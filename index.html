About Web D School

Yes! we are Unique

Web D School has been operational from the year 2014 & we have been conducting courses like UX UI Designing, Web Designing, Graphic Designing, Digital Marketing, Android app development, Editing, VFX & Animation. Through the years, our main focus had been in the classroom training, but we had been offering online courses too, all these years, to those students who reside outside Chennai.

But now, our focus on the online classes model has increased multifold, because of which we have completely revamped our training methodology, curriculum, knowledge sharing system etc.

We, Web D School, have gained immense reputation as the best training institute in the region, & more than 2000 past students would bear testimony to that fact.

We wish to get the same reputation in the online classroom space as well to be known as the best in the online training business. And we are in the right track to achieve that as well very shortly.
